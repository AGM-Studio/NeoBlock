# NeoBlock – The Ultimate OneBlock Experience, Reimagined

NeoBlock is a modern, fully customizable take on the classic OneBlock gameplay. Build your world from a single block—no datapacks, no premade worlds, just pure Minecraft magic tailored to *your* modpack.

![Minecraft, In one block, Called NeoBlock](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/09e8c190e58fa6221346a3adcbf9a0482d888537_0.webp)

> ⚠️ **Still in early development – your feedback matters!**  
> Help shape NeoBlock into the ultimate OneBlock mod.

---

## 🔥 Why NeoBlock?

- ✅ **No DataPacks Required**
- ✅ **No Prebuilt Worlds** – Just select the **NeoBlock** preset
- ✅ **Easy customization** via clean config files
- ✅ **Compatible with any mod** – perfect for modpacks

---

## 🎮 Core Features

1. **Tiered progression** with upgrade animations
2. **Auto-loading schematics** – bring your structures to life
3. **Smart wandering traders** – summon mobs you actually want
4. **Fully configurable block generation**
5. **Vanilla-friendly by default**, mod-friendly by design
